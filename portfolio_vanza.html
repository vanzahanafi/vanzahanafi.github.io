Equity Analysis

Quantitative research using multi-factor regression methodology to decode the drivers of UNVR's long-term underperformance on the Indonesia Stock Exchange.

IDX: UNVR — Consumer Goods FMCG

PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk

Multi-Factor Regression Analysis of Stock Price Determinants, Q1 2016 – Q4 2024

UNVR is one of Indonesia's most iconic consumer brands — yet its stock declined ~83% over 8 years, from peak Rp11,180 (Q4 2017) to Rp1,885 (Q4 2024). This research investigates the macroeconomic and fundamental drivers behind this persistent underperformance using quarterly panel data and OLS regression methodology.

Q1 2016 – Q4 2024 Research Period
36 Quarterly Observations
OLS Regression Methodology
4 Variables DER, EPS, Firm Size, IDR Rate

Bottom Line

UNVR's stock collapse is macro- and leverage-driven, not an earnings story. Recovery requires: (1) sustained IDR stabilization and (2) meaningful debt reduction. EPS-only analysis would have missed these structural risks entirely.
